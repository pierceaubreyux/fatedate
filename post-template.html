FateDate announces early access ahead of summer launch

FD
FateDate Team
12 March 2026
3 min read

We've been building FateDate quietly for the past two years. Today, we're opening the doors to early access — a limited group of members who'll be among the first to experience the app before we fully launch this summer.

This isn't a beta test. Early access members will have the full experience: verified profiles, the DateDiary, connection games, and our partner venues across London and Manchester. The only difference is you'll be getting in before everyone else.

Why we built this

Dating apps have a trust problem. You don't know who you're actually talking to, the incentives are all wrong, and the whole thing has been designed to keep you swiping rather than actually meeting people.

We wanted to build something that takes the process seriously — from the moment you sign up to the moment you walk into a venue for a first date. Every member on FateDate is ID-verified. Every venue is handpicked. Every interaction is designed to move things forward, not keep you stuck on your phone.

Dating should feel like the beginning of something, not a second job.

— FateDate founding team

What early access looks like

Early access opens in waves. The first wave is invite-only through our waitlist. If you're on it, you'll hear from us in the next few weeks with everything you need to get set up.

  • Full ID verification and profile setup
  • Access to connection-based matching games
  • DateDiary — browse and book dates at partner venues
  • Priority support from the founding team

If you're not on the waitlist yet, you can sign up now. We're working through it in order, and we're aiming to have everyone onboarded ahead of the public launch in July.

A note on venues

We'll be sharing more about our venue partners over the coming weeks. What we can say now is that we've been incredibly selective — these are places that have been chosen because they're genuinely great for a first date, not because they paid to be on the platform.

More soon. If you're press and want to speak to the team, get in touch at hello@fatedate.co.uk.
