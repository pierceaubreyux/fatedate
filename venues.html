FAQs

Questions we hear a lot

Is there a cost to become a venue partner?

No. We don't charge venues to be listed in the app and there's no ongoing fee. Our venues are chosen because they're genuinely great places for a date — not because they've paid to be there.

What types of venues do you work with?

We work with independent bars, restaurants, coffee shops, wine bars, rooftops, and cultural spaces. The common thread is atmosphere — we look for places that have something memorable about them, whether that's the décor, the menu, the music, or all three.

How do bookings actually work?

Members browse available venues in the app and choose where they'd like to go. We coordinate the booking on your behalf and you receive confirmation ahead of time. You don't need to change anything about how you operate — the dates show up like any other reservation.

How many dates can we expect?

We're honest about this — at launch, volumes will be modest while we build the member base. We'd rather set realistic expectations and exceed them than promise numbers we can't deliver on. What we can say is that every booking comes from a verified, committed member who's chosen your venue specifically.

Can we opt out or pause if needed?

Absolutely. You can pause your listing at any time — for a private event, a refurb, or just a quiet period. Just let us know and we'll handle it. There's no contract locking you in.

Where are you launching first?

London and Manchester in summer 2026, with more cities to follow. If you're based elsewhere, we'd still love to hear from you — we're building our waitlist for future cities now.
